This program is a simple bot that plays evil hangman. It won't pick a word, and will keep changing it's word based off your guessed to try and maximise it's chances of wining. It works by looping through a dictionary and seeing whether there are more words that don't have your letter of or more words that do, and makes it's choice of that peice of information. It will always pick the section that has more words, as the more words there are, the more options there are for you to miss.
